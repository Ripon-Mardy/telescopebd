Our Vision :: Telescope Bangladesh

  • 1. To provide and popularize the real understanding of astronomy among citizens of all ages in the country and to take the country to the advanced stage of astronomy and space science.
  • 2. To make available the instruments for the practice of astronomy in the country.
  • 3. To help the students who want to study Astronomy.
  • 4. And to establish an observatory for astronomy practice, research and sky observation in the country where the researchers of the country and abroad will use it free of cost :)
